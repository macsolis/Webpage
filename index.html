More About Afterglow Sunscreen Serum

Afterglow Skincare was born out of a love for music and beauty.Inspired by Taylor Swift’s song Afterglow, our brand captures the idea of finding light, hope, and protection in everyday life. We wanted to create a product that not only protects skin but also makes people feel radiant inside and out.

Our product, the Afterglow Sunscreen Serum SPF 60 PA ++++, is lightweigth sunscreen designed for modern skincare lovers. It combines high sun protection with serum texture, making it easy to wear daily under makeup or alone. Unlike heavy sunscreens, our serum absorbs quickly, leaving a soft, glowing finish.

Entering in the skincare industry was not easy. Barriers included high competition, product development costs, and building trust among consumers. However, our commitment to quality ingredients and unique inspiration helped us stand out. We focused on developing a sunscreen that feels luxurious while remaining affordable.

Our competitors include brands like Neutrogena, Biore, Belo, and Nivea. While they offer trusted sun protection, Afterglow differentiates itself by combining the science of skincare with the artistry of beauty and music. We don’t just sell sunscreen; we sell confidence and glow.

We set our prices at an accessible range of ₱350 per 50ml bottle, balancing quality and affordability. Consumers can trust that they are receiving excellent protection and skincare benefits at a fair price without overspending. Our aim is to encourage daily sun protection while giving customers a product they’ll actually love using.

“At Afterglow, our mission is to provide high-quality sun protection, lightweight, effective sunscreen that not only shields skin but also leaves a radiant glow. Inspired by music, beauty, and self-confidence, we create products that protect, give comfort, that allows everyone to feel confident while staying safe under the sun.”
